Our Story


Founded in 2017. Here at Design.B we are a locally owned business with a passion of turning dreams into reality through the use of cutting edge techonology. By digitally designing your jewellery, we are able to perfectley reimagine your ideas right down to the micrometre.

We understand how important and meaningful custom jewellery can be and it has to fit your lifestyle, so we offer personal one on one meetings with our designer of five years were he will be able to find the perfect match for you.
